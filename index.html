Light Meter LuMe

Light Meter for iPhone

A precision light meter for film and digital photographers.

LuMe turns your iPhone into a reliable handheld light meter — with spot, center-weighted, and matrix metering, film reciprocity correction, and tools made for medium and large format work.

What it does

LuMe reads light through your iPhone camera and recommends a correct exposure in real time. Pick a metering pattern, dial in any one of aperture, shutter, or ISO, and LuMe solves for the rest.

Designed for photographers who shoot film and want a serious light meter without carrying a separate device — and for digital shooters who want quick, reliable readings for off-camera work.

Download on the App Store iPhone · iOS 16.0+

Features

  • MeteringSpot, center-weighted, and matrix patterns. Multi-point spot averaging with on-tap distance via LiDAR.
  • ExposureLock any of aperture, shutter, or ISO and LuMe solves the rest. EV compensation dial with 1/3-stop snapping.
  • FilmReciprocity correction with Schwarzschild exponents for common film stocks.
  • Formats35mm full-frame, APS-C, half-frame, 110, medium format (6×4.5, 6×6, 6×7, 6×12, 6×17), Xpan, large format (4×5, 5×7).
  • ProfilesSave camera profiles — format, focal length, fixed aperture — and the viewfinder matches your camera's point of view.
  • CalculatorsDepth-of-field calculator (hyperfocal, near/far limits) and guide-number flash calculator.
  • ViewfinderHistogram, clipping warning, ND filter compensation, aspect-ratio crop, grid overlay.
  • LanguagesEnglish, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Dutch, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Swedish, Turkish, Arabic, and both Chinese variants.

Who it's for

If you shoot Tri-X on a Hasselblad, Portra on a Mamiya 7, or Velvia on a 4×5 — LuMe is built for you. If you shoot digital and need a fast meter for off-camera flash or video work, it works for that too.
