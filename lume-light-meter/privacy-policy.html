Lume Light Meter

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 9, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 9, 2026

The short version

Lume does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device. That's it.

This policy describes how Lume Light Meter ("Lume", "the app", "we") handles information when you use the app. We've written it to be as clear and direct as possible.

Information we collect

None. Lume does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics of any kind. The app operates entirely on your device. No accounts, no sign-in, no data sent to any server.

Camera access is used solely to measure ambient light in real time. No images or video are ever stored, transmitted, or processed outside of your device.

Third-party services

Lume does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with any third party.

Future changes

We may introduce optional, anonymous analytics in a future update to help us understand how the app is used and improve it. If and when we do, it will be clearly disclosed in an updated version of this policy, and you will have the choice to opt in or out. We will never collect personally identifiable information.

Children's privacy

Lume does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, this is satisfied by default.

Changes to this policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
