Lume Light Meter

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 9, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 12, 2026

The short version

Lume does not collect personal data. Camera data never leaves your device. The only third-party service used is RevenueCat, which processes anonymous purchase information to manage your subscription.

This policy describes how Lume Light Meter ("Lume", "the app", "we") handles information when you use the app. We've written it to be as clear and direct as possible.

Information we collect

None directly. Lume does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics of any kind. The app operates entirely on your device. No accounts, no sign-in, no data sent to our servers.

Camera access is used solely to measure ambient light in real time. No images or video are ever stored, transmitted, or processed outside of your device.

All settings and preferences (metering mode, ISO range, film presets, appearance, etc.) are stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage. None of this data is transmitted anywhere.

Third-party services

Lume uses RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes a limited set of non-personal data in order to verify and manage your subscription status:

  • An anonymous randomly-generated identifier (not linked to your name, email, or Apple ID)
  • Subscription and purchase status
  • App version, device type, and operating system version
  • Country code (for regional pricing)

RevenueCat does not receive your name, email address, camera data, photos, or location. Actual payment processing is handled entirely by Apple — Lume and RevenueCat never see your payment details.

For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

No advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs are used.

Children's privacy

Lume does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The only third-party data processing (RevenueCat) involves anonymous purchase identifiers with no personal information attached.

Changes to this policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
